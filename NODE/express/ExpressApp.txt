The app, created by `express()`, is just a handy function that handles different types of requests

app.listen() in Express is like telling your app to start listening for visitors on a specific address and port
    Syntax: 
        app.listen(port, callback)


app.use() in Express is used to check for request sent to the server
and in turn act accordingly. {It turns request into an object}
look for its basic in basics.js
